As with any decent eCommerce solution, there are costs to pay, but **Shopify fees** are well-rounded and more cost-effective than many other options out there. Shopify is very popular because it’s easy to set up and maintain as it’s a fully hosted SaaS (software as a service) platform, and the pricing is transparent.



This means your store can be launched as soon as you register and choose from the available [Shopify Plans](http://techlyser.com//).

o make the decision that’s best for you, here’s an overview of each plan and the additional **Shopify fees**you can expect.



### **Basic Shopify Fees for Each Pricing Plan**



Shopify offers five distinct pricing plans to merchants:



### 1. Shopify Lite



At $9 a month, it’s best for those who want to sell directly from their social media pages (Facebook, Squarespace, Tumblr) or WordPress. But, it can also be used for in-person selling via the Shopify POS app.



### 2. Basic Shopify



With a price of $29 a month, this plan is best for those who need a fully functional store. With this, you get 2 staff accounts and are able to list an unlimited number of products. This plan includes fraud analysis, abandoned cart recovery and a discount code engine too.



### 3. Shopify



This plan costs $79 a month and comes with everything the Basic plan has to offer. But it also adds POS functionality, and in-depth reporting on sales, marketing, and customer stats. This plan also has lower **Shopify fees** for transactions and credit cards when compared to Shopify Lite and Basic, and is best suited for those who plan to use POS and have a larger number of sales. We’ll discuss this more below.



### 4. Advanced Shopify



For $299 a month, this plan comes with all the features of the other plans, plus two additional things: advanced report builder and real-time carrier shipping. This plan is best suited for merchants with a large number of sales who use carriers to ship products.



### 5. Shopify Plus



For Enterprises only, Shopify Plus has flexible pricing depending on a business’s needs, but these are a minimum of $2,000 a month and up. It’s best suited for established businesses with very large budgets who want a tailored solution.



These are the basic monthly subscription prices, but additional **Shopify transaction fees**apply to each plan, based on how much you sell, payment providers you use, and types of payments you have.



### **A Look At Additional Shopify Fees**



Most merchants might not be aware that there are additional **Shopify merchant fees**that are charged on top of the basic monthly fee for each plan, split into two types:



1. *The****Shopify transaction fee*** Charged by *Shopify is* based on the payment gateway provider you are using (PayPal, Shopify Payments, Stripe, etc.)
2. *The credit card fee* is Charged by the payment gateway *provider*for each transaction. Note that the credit card fee changes based on your country.



How much these fees will ultimately cost you is based on:



* Different [Shopify Plans](http://techlyser.com//)
* The number of transactions
* Type of transaction
  + Online credit card transaction
  + In-person credit card transaction
  + Manual payments (bank deposit, cash on delivery, test/draft order)
  + POS orders
* Payment gateway provider you are using



### **Shopify Fees Breakdown for Each Plan**



To get a good idea of the actual monthly costs for each plan, here’s a breakdown that includes all applicable **Shopify Merchant fees**for each plan.
