ReConvert Upsell & Cross Sell is an app that helps you create a highly customisable ‘thank you’ landing page, which displays after a completed purchase.


![ReConvert](/images/blog/2021/04/screenshot-16-1024x335.jpg)



### **Features of the ReConvert Upsell & Cross-Sell app**



ReConvert Upsell & Cross Sell has an easy-to-use drag-and-drop page builder where different widgets and features can be added to your thank you page.



The app offers a range of widgets such as:



* discount pop-ups – with a timer to create urgency
* product recommendations – add upsell and cross-sell
* collecting people’s birthdays – tie these in with your [email marketing](https://techlyser.com/app-review-pathfinder/).
* order tracking – an important feature that will make people return to this page, leave comments etc
* add videos, images and social sharing – to create an engaging page
* call to action button – customisable to include important actions for your store
* post-purchase surveys – to find out what your customers think
* display reviews – can be used with the most popular reviews apps



There is also an analytics section to keep track of upsell and cross-sell purchases on your store after you install the app.



This app will suit any store wanting to increase conversion rates and sales.



### **Overall review**



The ReConvert Upsell & Cross-Sell app has over a thousand reviews and a 5-star rating in the Shopify app store, which makes it one of the most popular upsell apps.



Upsells and cross-sells are important to include in the buying process – but it’s also essential to have an engaging thank-you page as an effective way to increase repeat purchases.



What’s more. it’s a good place to add other marketing features to your store. A thank you page is always seen by a customer who has made a purchase, so is one of the few pages in your online store that gets a 100% guaranteed viewing.



This app has a lot of options and is very easy to install and use. The look of the widgets can be customised to fit your store, and all prompts and messages reworded.



The design of the features is clean and will fit with many of the Shopify themes. If you have any questions, the customer service team is friendly and very quick to respond with solutions.
